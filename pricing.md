# ProFinanceCast pricing

Single product, three tiers. No upsells beyond them.

| Tier | Price | Sage AI | Scenarios | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Free | $0 forever | 10 messages / month | 1 saved | Full forecast, full debt strategy, full goals. No card. No bank password. |
| Pro | $9 / month or $69 / year | 200 messages / month | Unlimited | Longer memory. Deeper analysis. Cancel any time. |
| Founders Lifetime | $149 once | 200 messages / month | Unlimited | Lifetime Pro entitlement. Limited to 500 seats. |

> **Heads-up on renewal:** Pro is currently sold as an access pass — at the end of the period, you re-purchase manually from the billing page to extend. Auto-renewal is on the roadmap for Q2. Founders Lifetime is unaffected.

## Free tier — what's included

- Ten-year net-worth forecast (full horizon, full resolution)
- Debt strategy (avalanche + snowball, line-by-line amortization)
- Goals projector (custom horizon, target tracking)
- Net-worth chart with monthly compounding
- Sage AI advisor — 10 messages a month
- Encrypted at rest. Calculations run in your browser.

## Pro tier — what Pro adds

- Sage AI advisor — 200 messages a month
- Longer Sage conversational memory
- Unlimited saved scenarios (Free is 1)
- Deeper analysis on dashboard insights
- Printable financial report card
- Priority email response from the team

## Founders Lifetime — what it is

- Pay $149 once. Pro features unlock for the lifetime of the account.
- Cap is 500 seats, no extensions.
- Same product as Pro at $9/month or $69/year — same features, same support, no exclusive content.
- Available while seats remain. After 500, the tier closes permanently. Pro at $9/month or $69/year continues unchanged.

## Payments

- Card payments are processed by PayPal — ProFinanceCast never sees or stores card details.
- All currencies presented in USD. Local-currency conversion is performed by PayPal at checkout.

## Refunds

- Pro monthly: cancel any time, no refund pro-rata.
- Pro annual: full refund within 14 days of purchase.
- Founders Lifetime: full refund within 14 days of purchase. After 14 days, non-refundable (the seat returns to the cap).
