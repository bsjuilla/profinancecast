Questions

How accurate is this?

It uses projected 2026 US federal brackets and the 2026/27 UK rates with standard deduction and personal allowance baked in. State tax for CA, NY, and IL is a flat approximation of the typical middle bracket. For tax filing use a qualified accountant; for forecasting it sits within a few percent of payroll.

Why isn't my state listed?

This first release covers California, New York, Texas, Florida, and Illinois. The full 50-state plus 12 UK region build is on the next sprint, paired with city-level cost-of-living pages.

Does this account for 401(k), HSA, or pension contributions?

Not in this version. The calculator assumes no pre-tax deductions so the gross figure is what you and your employer agreed. To model 401(k) or pension across a full forecast, use the Pro scenarios planner.

How is this different from Salary.com or Bankrate?

The math runs in your browser so we never see your salary. There is no signup, no email gate, and no advertising network. The upsell is a forecast that projects this take-home over the next ten years with raises and life events modelled in.

Is my salary data shared anywhere?

No. The calculation is pure JavaScript that runs on your device. Your salary never leaves the browser.
