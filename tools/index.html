Tools

Free calculators for thinking about your money.

Seven browser-only utilities from a boutique forecasting house. Each one is localised across global plus 8 markets — UK, Ireland, France, Germany, US, Canada, Australia, and Singapore. No signup, no affiliate links, and the math runs entirely on the reader's device.

Investing

Compound interest calculator

See how a starting amount plus monthly contributions grow under compound returns. Chart shows when the compounded portion starts pulling decisively ahead of what you put in. Localised across 9 markets with country-specific tax-wrapper context.

Property

Mortgage affordability calculator

Apply the 28/36 rule to a real income and debt profile to find a maximum affordable property price. Output includes the monthly payment for any target price, a housing and total debt-to-income ratio, and a clear afford/can't-afford verdict.

Retirement

FIRE date calculator

Project the date you reach financial independence under the 4% safe withdrawal rule. Surfaces unreachable scenarios honestly, runs a Coast FIRE check against the local state pension age, and plots your portfolio against the FI target across 40 years.

Debt

Debt snowball vs avalanche

Enter up to 8 debts with balances, APRs, and minimum payments. The calculator runs both strategies in parallel, shows months to debt-free and total interest paid under each, and flags the budget-below-minimums case with a precise shortfall.
