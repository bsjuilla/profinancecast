## Section 3 — Top 5 Perf Wins (Ranked)

**Bundle:** 5.2 MB across 41 WebP (avg 127 KB). Largest: cashflow-tide-band (425 KB). 100% lazy + async + width/height adoption. **No `fetchpriority="high"` on any image** — LCP candidates are un-prioritized.

### #1 — Add `fetchpriority="high"` to LCP-candidate images (30 min, biggest ROI)
Above-the-fold images on index.html (coastal-window, hero-ledger), dashboard, journal, about, help are tagged `loading="lazy"` — lazy on above-fold delays paint. Switch to `loading="eager"` + `fetchpriority="high"`. Expected LCP gain on 4G: **300-600 ms**.

### #2 — Defer 20+ blocking script tags
Supabase CDN unDeferred on 20 pages; Sentry on auth/about/take-home-pay; Chart.js on cash-forecast/net-worth. ~80 KB + 60 KB gzipped each, parser-blocking. Expected TTI gain: **400-800 ms**.

### #3 — Preload woff2 binaries above the fold
Currently preload Google Fonts CSS (two round-trips before first letter). Self-host (or preload) Fraunces 600 italic + Inter Tight 400. Expected FCP gain: **150-300 ms**.

### #4 — Re-compress 4 photos >200 KB
cashflow-tide-band (425 KB), onboarding-complete (211), portfolio-holdings (225), onboarding-welcome (268). Re-export at Q75 OR generate AVIF siblings (~30% smaller). Add `<source type="image/avif">` before WebP in `<picture>`. Saves ~400 KB (~8% bundle).

### #5 — Audit "first three images" above fold on dashboard/scenarios/report-card
Three images compete for connection slots in first viewport. Either collapse bento talismans to inline SVG and swap photo on `requestIdleCallback`, or move them 100px below the fold. Eliminates 3-way LCP contention.
