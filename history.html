Billing & subscription
Your live plan and payment method live on the billing page
Open billing →

Invoice history isn't archived inside the app — PayPal keeps the full receipt trail. From your billing page you can:

  • See your current plan (Free, Pro, or Premium) and the renewal date
  • Open your active PayPal subscription to view every past payment
  • Cancel, pause, or upgrade your plan

If you need a downloadable receipt for tax or expense filing, the PayPal email confirmations carry an official VAT-compliant copy.

Sage conversations
Persistent chat history isn't shipped yet
Open Sage →

Sage is the in-app AI advisor. Conversations are live in your current tab only — closing the tab clears the chat. Cross-session conversation history is on the roadmap but not yet built.

If you want to keep a particular answer, copy the reply text out of Sage before closing the tab.

When persistent Sage history ships, your past conversations will appear here automatically — no migration needed.
