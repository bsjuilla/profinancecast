v.Common questions

Plain answers, no fine print.

Do you connect to my bank account?

No, by design. Most personal-finance apps lure you in, sell your spending data, then go bankrupt with your credentials sitting in their database. ProFinanceCast does not connect to anything — you enter your numbers manually (or paste a CSV) and they live in your browser, encrypted with AES-256 against a Web Crypto API key that never leaves the device. We could not see your bank credentials even if subpoenaed: we never receive them.

How accurate is the forecast?

It is deterministic — same inputs always give the same forecast. The compound-growth curve uses the standard future-value formula: FV = PV × (1 + r)^n + PMT × ((1 + r)^n − 1) ÷ r. Real inflation comes from the World Bank consumer-price index for your country (refreshed quarterly). The accuracy is a function of how honest you are with your inputs — not of any opaque model. ProFinanceCast does not predict the stock market.

What happens to my data if I cancel Pro?

Your data stays in your browser locally — only the Pro features (multi-scenario, salary calc, Quarterly Report Card) gate back to Free. The forecast keeps running on the same numbers. Export everything to CSV or JSON from Settings in one click, or wipe the entire local store with a second click. The thirty-day refund applies regardless of usage; no email exchange required.

Is there a free trial of Pro?

The Free plan covers the core ten-year forecast forever — no time limit, no degradation, no upsell prompts after month three. Trials are most often dark patterns, so we do not run them. If you need Pro for a single month — modelling a single job offer, say — subscribe at $9, cancel before renewal, and the next charge does not happen. Any paid plan is refundable within thirty days from Settings.

Do you sell my data?

No. The forecast runs in your browser, not on our servers — the math literally happens on the device you're holding. The only thing we transmit on this homepage is a single anonymous page-view (Cloudflare Web Analytics, no cookies, no IP retention beyond aggregation). There is no ad network, no data broker, no behavioural profile. Read the full data-handling note in privacy.

What if I cancel?

You keep your data. Cancellation gates the Pro features (multi-scenario, salary comparison, report card, higher Sage limits) and leaves the Free forecast running on the same numbers. Export everything to CSV or JSON from Settings — one button, no support ticket. If you cancel within 30 days of any paid charge, the charge reverses; no email exchange required.

Is this tax or investment advice?

No. ProFinanceCast is a deterministic forecasting tool — math, not advice. It is not a registered investment adviser, broker-dealer, or fiduciary, and nothing on the page is a recommendation to buy, sell, or hold any specific instrument. Tax figures use published statutory rates for your country; consult a licensed adviser before acting on any number. The full methodology shows every formula and assumption.

Who built ProFinanceCast?

An independent forecasting house based in Europe. ProFinanceCast takes no outside money, sells no user data, runs no advertising network, and does not connect to a single bank. The product — three tiers, one ten-year compound-growth model — is the entirety of the offer; there is no consulting arm, no managed accounts, no upsell to a paid advisor. Read the methodology →

What happens when 500 Founders Lifetime seats are reached?

Once all 500 Founders Lifetime seats are claimed, the tier closes permanently — there is no extension, no waitlist, and no second cohort at a higher price. Pro at $9 monthly or $69 annual continues unchanged. The seat counter on the pricing page is the live source of truth — it polls the same Stripe webhook that gates the checkout, so it cannot drift from the actual claim count.
