FAQ

Common questions, plain answers.

Do you connect to my bank account?

No. ProFinanceCast is intentionally bank-decoupled. You enter your numbers manually (or paste a CSV) and they live in your browser, encrypted. We never see, store, or sell your bank credentials.

How accurate is the forecast?

It's a deterministic projection from your inputs — same inputs always give the same forecast. We layer on inflation data from the World Bank for your country and let you tweak assumptions in scenarios.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data stays in your browser locally even after you cancel a paid plan — only the Pro features get gated. You can export or wipe everything any time from Settings.

Can I switch from Pro to Premium later?

Yes. Upgrade from your billing page any time — the price is prorated by PayPal automatically.

Is there a free trial of Pro?

Free forever covers the core forecast. We don't do limited trials — if you want Pro for one month, subscribe and cancel before renewal.
