Common questions

Plain answers, no fine print.

Do you connect to my bank account?

No, by design. Most "personal finance" apps lure you in, sell your spending data, then go bankrupt with your bank credentials sitting in their database. We don't connect to anything — you enter your numbers manually (or paste a CSV) and they live in your browser, encrypted. We never see, store, or sell your bank credentials. We never could.

How accurate is the forecast?

It's deterministic — same inputs always give the same forecast. We're not predicting the stock market. We're compounding what you tell us about your income, debts, and savings rate, and layering on real inflation data from the World Bank for your country. The accuracy is a function of how honest you are with your inputs.

What happens to my data if I cancel Pro?

Your data stays in your browser locally — only the Pro features (scenarios, salary calc, report card) get gated again. Free features keep working. You can export everything to CSV/JSON or wipe it all in one click from Settings.

Is there a free trial of Pro?

The Free plan covers the core forecast forever — no time limit. We don't run "trials" because most trials are confusing dark patterns. If you want Pro for a month, subscribe and cancel before renewal. Cancellation takes 5 seconds.

Who built this?

One developer (the reason it's still cheap). Built because every personal finance tool either connects to your bank (creepy) or only shows you the past (useless). I wanted to see the future.
