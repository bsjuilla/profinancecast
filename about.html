About ProFinanceCast

ProFinanceCast is built by independent professionals in Europe; private by design; published in the editorial register of a private-bank quarterly letter. The product projects ten years of net worth, the month a household becomes debt-free, and the goals it will meet — without connecting to any bank account. Calculations run in the reader's browser. Nothing in the projection leaves the device unencrypted, and the projection is encrypted before it touches any server.

There is one product, three tiers — Free, Pro at $9 a month or $69 a year, Founders Lifetime at $149 limited to 500 seats — and no upsells beyond them. ProFinanceCast does not store bank credentials, does not sell user data, and is not affiliated with any institution it forecasts against.

Methodology

Each forecast is built from four user inputs — monthly income, monthly expenses, monthly savings, and total debt — and three macroeconomic assumptions: an inflation rate, a long-run real return, and a static income trajectory unless the reader varies it on the dashboard.

The compound-growth curve uses the standard future-value formula:

FV = PV × (1 + r)^n + PMT × ((1 + r)^n − 1) ÷ r

where PV is starting net worth, PMT is monthly savings, r is the monthly real return (annual real return divided by twelve), and n is the number of months in the horizon. Inflation is applied to expenses each month using a published consumer-price index for the reader's reporting currency, fetched fresh on each forecast and cached for one day.

Debt amortization is computed line by line: each debt carries its own balance, rate, and minimum payment, and ProFinanceCast simulates either the avalanche order (highest interest rate first) or the snowball order (lowest balance first), whichever the reader selects on the dashboard. The same numbers in produce the same forecast out, every time. There is no smoothing, no scenario-mixing, and no proprietary "score" that hides its math.

Primary sources

  • World Bank — consumer-price index, country-level (api.worldbank.org)
  • Federal Reserve Economic Data — long-run real return baseline (fred.stlouisfed.org)
  • European Central Bank — harmonized index of consumer prices (ecb.europa.eu)

What we are not

  • Not a bank. ProFinanceCast does not hold deposits, issue credit, or move money.
  • Not a registered investment adviser. Forecasts are educational projections, not personalized advice. Consult a licensed adviser before acting on any figure shown.
  • Not a broker-dealer or fiduciary. No transactions are facilitated through this product.
  • Not a tax advisor. Tax assumptions are illustrative; jurisdictional rules are the reader's responsibility.
  • Not affiliated with any bank, brokerage, asset manager, or other institution that may appear in the forecast.
  • Not available in jurisdictions where the operation of an unlicensed financial-projection tool is restricted. Local applicability is the reader's responsibility.
